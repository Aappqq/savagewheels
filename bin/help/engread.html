    About this game


    Hey, many thanks for downloading Savage Wheels.

    Savage Wheels is a car-crashing game, where up to four vehicles take part in a deadly tournament.The goal is to eleminate the other derby contestants.You can choose between a variety of vehicles, each with its unique characteristics: speed,acceleration,armour,damage...and driver!Gameplay modes include a campaign, hotseat mode between two human players or melee mode where other vehicles are controled by AI.

    It is not easy to compete with three dimensional games these days, so we hope you will enjoy this 2D one and play it in your spare time.

    Game controls and Hints


    Player 1 keys:
    arrow keys to move

       UP (accelerate up)
LEFT (turn left)   RIGHT (turn right)
      DOWN (back gear)

RIGHT CTRL - to place landmines
DELETE - self destruction
    		Player 2 keys:
    
	  R (accelerate up)
  D (turn left)   G (turn right) 
        F (back gear)


LEFT CTRL - to place landmines
TAB - self destruction

    You can change the terrain by pressing F5.
    During the game you can also turn on/off change the music with the F4 key.
    To turn on/off the shadows use F9 key.

    The blue bar is your armour(hitpoints) indicator. Should it fall below half its size
    your vehicle should look smashed.
    The red bar is your anger indicator. The more anger you get the more damage your vehicle will do.
    The white numbers are your frags.


    Some hints and facts:
  • If you self destruct, you will loose a frag.
  • Any inactive vehicle is to be destroyed within 5-7 seconds.
  • Inactive destruction does not take frags.
  • Landmines are pretty useful if there's an enemy behind your back.
  • You will not suffer any damage if you run over a landmine you put.
  • To do full damage hit enemies with your highest speed.
  • If you hit an enemy at low speed you will not do your full anger.



    When you run over a bug, you get a specific bonus.A good hint is always
    to run for a frag, or anger giving bug.Below is a table of them bugs and the
    bonuses they give.


    +1 frag.
    Gives anger.
    Repairs your vehicle.
    Speeds up your vehicle.
    Gives +3 Landmines.






    Troubleshooting


    If the game screen flickers or it runs too slow, you should try run it with the -hw command line.Here is how to do that:
    First click the right button on the sw.exe file and select Create Shortcut.Then click the right button on the shortcut's
    Properties option.In the Target area, after the path name, leave a space and add -hw and then click Apply or OK.
    Run the game from the shortcut.
